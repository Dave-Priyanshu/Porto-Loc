FAQ

What's included in the Free plan?

Projects in the Free plan have access to 10 CMS collections, 1000 pages, 5MB file uploads, and a free locale to try. You can use this to explore Framer or to design templates. To use a custom domain, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan.

Which plan is right for me?

Our Free plan is ideal for non-commercial use, the Mini and Basic plans cater to students, freelancers, and small teams, and the Business plans are designed for teams or agencies with more complex needs. All plans come with a custom domain, annual billing, dedicated support, and the ability to upgrade or downgrade at any time.

What happens to the Startup and Scaleup plans?

The Startup plan is renamed to Launch, and the Scaleup plan to Scale. The only difference between them is the price and the features they come with.

How do I add extra editors?

Editors can be added per editor, per month, or per year on any plan, and can be added to your workspace or projects at any time. We will notify you when an editor is added to a billing cycle, so you won’t be charged until the end of the cycle. Please note, editors will be charged per editor according to the highest active plan.

What happens if I go over a limit?

When you reach certain limits, we will ask you to upgrade your plan in Framer. For more information on limits and form restrictions, please visit our help center or contact support directly.

What if I only need open-source side projects instead of a full project?

We can be flexible with our limits for open-source or non-commercial side projects. Please contact us to discuss the options.

What payment methods do you offer?

We accept payments via major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, and American Express. We also offer billing options, including credit card billing options, for Enterprise plans.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a full refund within 14 days of your annual subscription purchase if you live in the EU or Turkey. You are also eligible for a full refund if the subscription is canceled within 14 days of purchase. Please contact our support team, and they will cancel the subscription and process the refund.
